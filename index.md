# Privacy Policy — Kidtronica

*Last updated: May 2026*

## Overview

Kidtronica does not collect, store, or share any personal information. 
This policy explains what data the app uses and how.

## Data We Collect

**None.** Kidtronica does not collect personal data, does not require 
an account, and does not track you across apps or websites.

## Third-Party Services

Kidtronica uses the following Apple services, each governed by 
Apple's own Privacy Policy:

- **Game Center** — for leaderboards and achievements. Your Game 
  Center profile is managed entirely by Apple.
- **In-App Purchases** — processed securely by Apple. Kidtronica 
  never sees your payment details.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Kidtronica stores the following locally on your device only:

- Your songs and projects
- Your progress, streaks, and achievement status

This data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers.

## Children's Privacy

Kidtronica is designed for users aged 9 and older. The app does not 
collect any personal information from any user, including children.
