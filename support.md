## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I make a beat?**
Pick a genre from the top bar, then tap CREATE on any track. 
Kidtronica fills it with a pattern that fits the genre. Tap CREATE 
again to cycle through variations. Hit play to hear it all together.

**How do I unlock more genres?**
Tap any locked genre to preview it. To unlock all genres permanently, 
tap Unlock All Genres — it's a one-time purchase with no subscription.

**My purchase isn't showing up. What do I do?**
Go to Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases. If the problem 
continues, contact us with your Apple ID and we'll help sort it out.

**How do I save my song?**
Tap the save icon in the toolbar. Your songs are stored locally on 
your device.

**How do I share my song?**
Tap the export button to generate a video of your beat. You can share 
it directly to Instagram, TikTok, iMessage, or anywhere else from 
there.

**How do I connect to Game Center?**
Game Center connects automatically using your Apple ID. Make sure 
you're signed into Game Center in your iPhone Settings.

**The app crashed. What should I do?**
Email us at ronan.conlon@gmail.com with your device model and iOS 
version and we'll look into it.
